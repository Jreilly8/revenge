== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload and Choose File, then select the theme's ZIP file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I change the color Logo? =

You can change the logo of your site easily using Revenge.

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Customize.
2. Now you will see the Customizer and a tab called 'Images'. Click this tab.
3. You can now change your logo by uploading your own. You can preview the change in the Customizer.
4. Once you are happy with your logo changes you can click save and your changes will be reflected on your live site.

= How do I change the jumbotron background image? =

You can change the jumbotron background image? on the index of your site easily using Revenge.

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Customize.
2. Now you will see the Customizer and a tab called 'Images'. Click this tab.
3. You can now change edit the Jumbotron Background by uploading your own. You can preview the change in the Customizer.
4. Once you are happy with your new image you can click save and your changes will be reflected on your live site.

= How do I add the Social Links? =

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Customize.
2. Now you will see the Customizer and a tab called 'Socail Links'. Click this tab.
3. You can now add your links by pasting your own.
4. Once you are done adding links click save and your changes will be reflected on your live site.

= How do I change backround color? =

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Customize.
2. Now you will see the Customizer and a tab called 'Colors'. Click this tab.
3. You can now change your 'Background Gradient' color by using the color picker.
4. Once you are done changing color click save and your changes will be reflected on your live site.
